UNC FLYING SILK

We Are UNC's Only Traditional Dance Group.

We aim to provide UNC students who are interested in Chinese culture with an outlet for expression and to educate the student community on the traditional aspects of Chinese dance. Each of our pieces are thoughtfully choreographed by students on the team and incorporate elements of Chinese history, including the use of Han ribbon and silk fans. We host auditions towards the beginning of every school semester for those interested in trying out. All genders, ethnicities, and skill levels are welcome!
